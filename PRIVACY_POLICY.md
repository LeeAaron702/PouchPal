# Privacy Policy for PouchPal

**Last Updated: January 13, 2026**

## Overview

PouchPal ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

PouchPal stores the following information **only on your device**:

- **Usage logs**: Timestamps and quantities of items you track
- **App preferences**: Your settings such as daily limits, notification preferences, and custom labels
- **Statistical data**: Aggregated counts and averages calculated from your logs

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.**

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Health or medical information
- Payment or financial information
- Browsing history
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Any data from other apps on your device

## How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to:

1. Display your daily, weekly, and monthly usage statistics
2. Send optional local notifications about daily limits (if enabled)
3. Power home screen widgets showing your current count
4. Export your data in CSV format (only when you explicitly request it)

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework
- Data is protected by your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
- If you use iCloud backup, your app data may be included in your encrypted backup
- We have no access to your data at any time

## Third-Party Services

PouchPal does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Widgets and App Extensions

Our home screen widget accesses your usage data through a secure App Group container shared only between the main app and the widget. This data never leaves your device.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data remains on your device until you delete it
- You can delete all data at any time through Settings > Delete All Data
- Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device

## Children's Privacy

PouchPal does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain age-restricted content.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access all your data (it's on your device)
- Export your data in CSV format
- Delete all your data at any time
- Use the app without providing any personal information
